Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

For Job Seekers

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is easy, click on the "Sign Up" button and fill in the required information including your name, email address, and a password. Once you submit the form, you will receive a confirmation email. Click the link in the email to verify your account.

What types of jobs does the platform offer

The website offers a variety of jobs from various industries ie Health,Education,Transport etc

How do I search for jobs?

Once you have created an account and logged in, you can search for jobs by using the search bar on the homepage. You can filter jobs by location, industry, job type, and more.

How do I apply for a job?

After finding a job that interests you, click on the job listing to view the details. If you meet the requirements, click the "Apply" button. You will be prompted to upload your resume and cover letter.Most jobs have a link to the employers website.

Can I save my searches?

Yes, you can save job searches by clicking the "Save" button on the job details page and later view them in your profile.

For Employers

How do I create an employer account?

To create an employer account, click on the "Employer Sign Up" button in the homepage. Fill in the required information including your company name, email address, and a password. Verify your email address by clicking the link in the confirmation email.

How do I search for candidates?

You can search for candidates by clicking on the "Search" tab in your employer dashboard. Use the search filters to narrow down candidates by skills, experience, location, and other criteria.

General Questions

Is there a cost to use the platform?

Our services are completely free for the job seekers.

How can I contact customer support?

If you need assistance, you can contact our customer support team by clicking on the "Contact Us" button at the top of the page. You can also reach us via email at supportjobaccessplatform@gmail.com or call us at (256) 0772546727.

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we will send you a link to reset your password. Follow the instructions in the email to set a new password.
